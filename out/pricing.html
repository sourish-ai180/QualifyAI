Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as a 'Lead'?

A lead is any unique prospect who starts a conversation with your AI Qualifier. We only count leads that finish at least 3 messages of the conversation.

Can I use my own domain?

Yes! Professional and Agency plans allow you to host the intake on your own subdomains (e.g., qualify.yourdomain.com).

Does it integrate with my CRM?

Currently, we integrate with Calendly and provide Webhook support. Direct HubSpot and Pipedrive integrations are launching in Q1 2025.

Is there a limit on how much the AI can chat?

Each lead can have an unlimited back-and-forth within the intake session. Your monthly limit only applies to the number of unique leads.
